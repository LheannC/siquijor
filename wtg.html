Pitogo Cliff

It’s a hidden gem that offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere, perfect for those looking to escape the crowds and enjoy nature.

  • Location: Pitogo Falls is located in the town of Maria, on the eastern side of Siquijor Island.
  • Accessibility: The falls are relatively easy to reach, with a short hike through lush greenery.
  • Ambiance: Unlike the more popular Cambugahay Falls, Pitogo Falls is quieter and less developed, making it ideal for a peaceful retreat.

Salagdoong Beach

Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sand, and thrilling cliff diving spots, it's a must-visit destination for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.

  • Location: Salagdoong Beach is located in the municipality of Maria, on the northeastern coast of Siquijor Island.
  • Accessibility: The beach is part of the Salagdoong Forest Resort, which is well-maintained and easily accessible by motorbike, car, or tricycle.
  • Features: The beach is famous for its cliff diving platforms, natural rock formations, and vibrant marine life.

San Isidro Labrador Church

One of the most iconic and historically significant landmarks on the island of Siquijor, Philippines.

  • Location: Lazi, Siquijor, Philippines.
  • Historical Significance: Built in the 19th century, the church and convent are recognized as National Cultural Treasures by the National Museum of the Philippines.
  • Architectural Style: The structures showcase Spanish colonial architecture, with coral stones and hardwood used in their construction.

Cambugahay Falls

Known for its multi-tiered cascades, turquoise pools, and lush surroundings, it’s a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  • Location: Located in the town of Lazi, Siquijor, Philippines.
  • Features: Cambugahay Falls is a three-tiered waterfall with crystal-clear turquoise pools, surrounded by lush greenery and natural rock formations.
  • Accessibility: The falls are easily accessible, with a short hike down a staircase from the main road.

Paliton Beach

Known for its powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and serene atmosphere, it’s a must-visit destination for travelers seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

  • Location: Located in the town of San Juan, on the western coast of Siquijor Island.
  • Features: Paliton Beach is famous for its pristine white sand, shallow turquoise waters, and palm-fringed shoreline.
  • Ambiance: The beach is less crowded compared to other tourist spots, making it ideal for those looking for a peaceful escape.

Lugnason Falls

Tucked away in a lush, tropical setting, this waterfall offers a serene and off-the-beaten-path experience for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

  • Location: Located in the town of Lazi, Siquijor, Philippines.
  • Features: Lugnason Falls is a single-tier waterfall with a natural pool at its base, surrounded by dense foliage and rocky formations.
  • Ambiance: Unlike the more popular Cambugahay Falls, Lugnason Falls is quieter and less developed, making it ideal for those looking to escape the crowds.
